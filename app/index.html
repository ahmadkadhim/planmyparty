PLAN MY PARTY

Putting The Party In Your Hands

After 5 years of hosting high school parties exclusively, PlanMyProm is revolutionizing the prom industry. We're automating ticket purchasing and limo booking while enabling students to share their most memorable moments in one mobile interface. Since 2010, MyProm Inc. has been the solution to the many stresses of prom time. We have hosted 50+ events at some of Toronto's most sought-after nightclubs and yachts, and organized limousines for 4,000+ students. From promposal through to after prom, PlanMyProm is here to capture your most memorable experiences.

  • Purchase Tickets

    Find and buy tickets to the best after prom parties in the city. See who’s attending and details about the venue.

  • Organize Limos

    Easiest way to organize your group and add members. Always on time. Track your limo the night of prom. Money back guarantee.

  • Share Memories

    Share photos and videos. Caption and comment. Tag by timeline. View your school or every school. Streamed and projected live at your event. Share on social media.
